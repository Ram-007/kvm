Preparatory

Kali Vidya Mandir prepares their students to the best of tradition and innovation in education. Our program includes reading and language arts, mathematics and social studies, which is taught by grade- level teachers within the classroom. In the lower Elementary the emphasis is on skill building which lays the foundation for the focus on independent learning that takes place in the upper Elementary. Our program is designed to prepare students for success in rigorous secondary schools like R.K. Mission, Sainik School, Navodaya Vidyalaya, Sri Sathya Sai Vidyapeeth, BITS Pilani, R.I.M.C etc. We help our students develop core competencies in accessing, evaluating, and synthesizing information, as well as encouraging them to think critically and creatively. We seek to foster an attitude of excitement for learning, curiosity and exploration that will be a lifelong process.

CBSE Curriculum

The central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) is a national level board of education in India for Public and Private schools. We follow the standard CBSE curriculum combined with our philosophy of focusing on our students and their learning needs.
